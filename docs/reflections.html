2 Reflections

Shattered mirror reflections seeded down the street along the night. Tall trees branches bare beneath my feet run clouds. And like a flower that opens the night a swatch of sky in a ray of sunshine: a sketch of light melts down the street turns into a shadow, a Flash of elsewhere. And I, who are just a reflection in this nothing copyright, another myself. An image skewed. A pebble thrown among puddles scattered my circulars horizons.
